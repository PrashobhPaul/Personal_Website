title: Why I keep coming back to *rule-based* engines
date: 2026-04-28
category: First Principles

The ML-enhanced version of ProfitPilot beats the rule-based version on average. The rule-based version beats it on the failure modes I care about.

In regulated domains, predictable wrongness usually beats unpredictable rightness. That's not a slogan — it's a procurement reality. A regulator can sign off on a system whose failure modes are bounded and known. They cannot sign off on one whose failure modes shift with each retraining.
