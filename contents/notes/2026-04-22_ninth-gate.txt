title: The *9th gate* is for the system, not the human
date: 2026-04-22
category: Architecture

Originally added a final HITL gate at the docs stage thinking humans needed it. Turns out the gate exists so the system can stop itself — the human is just the witness.

Reframed the whole governance model after that. The gates aren't there because humans don't trust the agents. They're there because the system needs deterministic stop conditions, and a human signature is the cheapest way to manufacture one.
